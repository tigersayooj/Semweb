About Our Project

Resource Description Framework is a way for search engine machines/crawlers to make sense of content in your HTML.

If you're like most web designers or SEOs, you might find RDFa, a little difficult to grasp at first. That's why we decided to help you quickly build and get started with RDFa/microdata.

We've included some of the most popular schemas for you to create. After you create a schema, you can copy and paste the code to your site or add additional item properties to it.
